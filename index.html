Our Story

OPAL OUTFITTERS was founded by Sudais in 2018 with a vision of providing premium fashion products with quality, style, and affordability. Since its establishment, the brand has continuously grown and earned customer trust through excellent products and service.

Today OPAL OUTFITTERS stands as a modern fashion destination committed to delivering the latest trends and exceptional shopping experiences.

Who We Are

OPAL OUTFITTERS is a premium fashion brand dedicated to delivering high-quality clothing, shoes, and accessories. Founded by Sudais, our mission is to make luxury fashion accessible without compromising on quality or style.

Privacy Policy

We collect only the data necessary to process your orders. Your information is never sold to third parties. All data is stored securely and used solely for order fulfilment and communication purposes.

Terms & Conditions

By using OPAL OUTFITTERS you agree to our terms. All products are subject to availability. Prices may change without notice. We reserve the right to cancel orders in cases of pricing errors.

Refund Policy

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for unused, undamaged items with original packaging. Sale items are non-refundable. Contact us to initiate a return.

Shipping Policy

Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days. Delivery within Pakistan: 3–5 days. International: 7–14 days. Free shipping on orders above PKR 5000.
